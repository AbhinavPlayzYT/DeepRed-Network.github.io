Out Top Minigames:

There are alot of minigames we offer in the DeepRed - Network! Every one of them are equally fun and enjoyable. But the top 3 gamemodes are:

Factions

The Factions is a really popular and fun game in the minecraft community. Each faction is a group of players who can claim land to make their bases and hoard stuff there, they can make alliances as well as enter in war with other factions, the bases are usually built well hidden and well protected, the plugin itself provides a layer of protection for the land claimed by the factions. There are kits for you to start with or buy, they also have OP custom enchants but u gotta get grinding for them.

Creative

The Creative minigame is a minigame that provides plots to players and to build in those plots in creative mode. You can get really creative with the builds and show other people. Explore and learn from other player's builds to make your own one. There's a huge community so you wont run out of ideas ;)

Bedwars [COMING SOON]

In Bed Wars, players spawn on an island and collect resources to obtain items such as swords, blocks, tools, and other special items. Players also have a bed that they must protect, as the bed breaking results in players no longer being able to respawn. There are 4 gamemodes: Solos(No Teams), Duo(Teams of 2), Trios(Teams of 3) and Quads(Teams of 4)
